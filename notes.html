Notes for yourself

If you have a widescreen laptop that projects only the left part of the screen, you can use the right edge of the screen for notes, like this: 

<div id="notes">
Put any notes here you want to see
while you're speaking.
</div>
The audience won't see the notes, so you can remind yourself of jokes or stories you want to tell, or important points you don't want to forget to mention.
